Primary source: UNH-UITF BeamNetUS Proposal.docx in /Users/takwirira/Desktop/TOPAS/UITF.
Relevant proposal facts used:
- 8 MeV electron beam at UITF with current range 1-10 uA.
- 250 mg target material, solid ammonia including ND3, held in variable-temperature cryostat.
- Prior holder dimensions used for simulation/proposal comparison: length 6.5 cm, radius 1.42 cm, interaction area 6.42 cm2.
- Each irradiation must be done twice, front and back, because energy deposition drops with depth.
- Warm irradiation target: about 1e17 e-/cm2 at 10 uA, 2.85 hours per side.
- Cold irradiation target: about 5e15 e-/cm2 at 1 uA, 1.42 hours per side.
- Beam spot/raster radius is about 14 mm.
- Target materials are approximately 1 mm radius solidified spheres, but single-bead dose is not listed as a primary or secondary measurement.
